Scaffold Alarms (Scaff Tab)

  • View Systems: See all scaffold alarm systems with hire status, customer info, and invoice details
  • Filter Systems: Filter by On Hire, Off Hire, or view all systems
  • Add New System: Click "Add Scaff System" to register a new scaffold alarm
  • Edit System: Click the ‚úèÔ∏è button to update system details including:
    • Company/Customer Name (who hired it)
    • Site Location (friendly name like "Royal Close")
    • Full address, postcode, and contact details
    • Extra sensors, ARC settings, and hire dates
  • Change Hire Status: Click "Change" to put systems on hire or take them off hire
  • View History: Click üìã to see complete rental history including all invoices and changes
  • Mark Invoice Sent: Click the green "üìÑ Sent" button to:
    • Record invoice sent date
    • Add ServiceM8 invoice number
    • Automatically calculate 4-week invoice amount
    • Set next invoice due date (28 days later)

Maintenance (Maint Tab)

  • View Customers: See all customers with their alarm systems
  • Add Customer: Register new customers with contact details
  • Edit Customer: Update customer information
  • Delete Customer: Remove customers (only if they have no active systems)
  • View Systems: Expand customer rows to see all their alarm systems

NSI Tab

  • Manage Customers: Similar to Maint tab with customer management
  • Track NSI Systems: View and manage NSI-certified alarm systems

Logs Tab

  • Voice Command History: See all voice commands you've used
  • Track Actions: Review what the voice assistant did
  • Clear Logs: Remove old log entries

üí° Quick Tips

  • Invoice Tracking: Systems show "Due in X days" or "Overdue by X days" for easy tracking
  • Weekly Costs: ¬£10/week base + ¬£2/week per extra sensor
  • 4-Week Billing: Invoices are automatically calculated for 4-week periods
  • Color Coding: On-hire systems are green, off-hire are gray
  • Statistics: The top of the Scaff page shows total active systems, weekly/monthly revenue, and overdue count
  • Mobile Friendly: All features work on phone and tablet

üé§ Voice Control Commands

You can control the app using voice commands. Click the microphone icon to start speaking, then click it again to send your command.

Counting Systems

  • "How many systems are on hire?"
  • "How many scaffold alarms in stock?"
  • "How many systems are off hire?"
  • "What systems are in stock?"

Finding Systems

  • "Where is P20?" - Find a system's location
  • "How many scaffold alarms on church buildings?"
  • "How many systems in Oxford?"
  • "How many systems outside Oxford?"

Revenue Information

  • "What's the total monthly revenue?"
  • "Calculate weekly revenue"
  • "What's the average revenue per scaffold system?"

Changing Hire Status

  • "Change P1 to off hire"
  • "Put P13 on hire"
  • "The one at Royal Close Chippenham has come off hire"
  • "The scaffold alarm at the church came off hire last Thursday"

Installing & Updating Systems

  • "I've installed P7 at St Margarets Church for 1st Choice Scaffolding"
  • "Update P9 site contact to Mark Murphy"
  • "Change P9 customer to First Choice Scaffolding"
  • "Update the site contact for the one at Oxford"

Maintenance Queries

  • "Have I got any maintenance due this month?"
  • "How many maintenance do I have due next month?"
  • "How many maintenance in November?"
  • "How many customers do I have?"
  • "How many NSI customers?"

Analysis Questions

  • "What's my busiest month for maintenances?"
  • "How many maintenance do I have to do each year for NSI approved systems?"

Voice Control Tips

  • Speak clearly and at a normal pace
  • Use system numbers like "P13" or "P16"
  • You can ask natural questions like "How many on hire?"
  • The assistant will confirm actions before making changes
  • Check the Logs tab to see what commands you've used
